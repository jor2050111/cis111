# HealthyLife Platform Audit

**Status:** Fictional instructional scenario

## Decision

HealthyLife must respond to evidence that its recommendation and advertising practices may harm young users.

## Evidence

* The algorithm escalates weight-loss content toward extreme dieting material.
* Paid supplement posts are not labeled clearly.
* Volunteer moderators reported harmful content.
* HealthyLife receives 35% of its revenue from supplement partnerships.

## Student Task

Use the evidence with the chapter's named ethical frameworks, laws,
policies, and stakeholder tests. State any assumption you add. Do not
treat a missing fact as proof.
