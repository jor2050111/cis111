# MidState Credential Breach

**Status:** Fictional instructional scenario

## Decision

MidState Community College must respond to a phishing campaign that exposed faculty credentials and student records.

## Evidence

* The phishing message reached about 200 faculty members.
* Thirty-two faculty members entered credentials.
* Six compromised accounts accessed the student information system.
* About 4,500 student records may have been exposed.
