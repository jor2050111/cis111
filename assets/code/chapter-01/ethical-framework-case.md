# Predictive Policing Algorithm

**Status:** Fictional instructional scenario

## Decision

Metro City must decide whether to continue, modify, or cancel CrimeMap Pro.

## Evidence

* Property crime fell 15% in targeted areas after six months.
* Seventy percent of added patrols went to predominantly Black and Latino neighborhoods.
* Arrests rose 35% in targeted neighborhoods while other neighborhoods stayed flat.
* The vendor will not permit an independent algorithm audit.

## Student Task

Use the evidence with the chapter's named ethical frameworks, laws,
policies, and stakeholder tests. State any assumption you add. Do not
treat a missing fact as proof.
