# Capstone Scenario Builder

**Status:** Fictional instructional scenario

## Decision

Use this guide to define one ethical decision with no obvious answer.

## Evidence

* Name one organization and one decision-maker.
* Include at least three stakeholder groups.
* Provide four facts that shape the decision.
* Connect the case to four chapters, two ethical frameworks, one professional code, and one legal or policy concept.

## Student Task

Use the evidence with the chapter's named ethical frameworks, laws,
policies, and stakeholder tests. State any assumption you add. Do not
treat a missing fact as proof.
