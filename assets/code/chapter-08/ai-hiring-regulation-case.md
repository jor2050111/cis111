# TalentScreen International Expansion

**Status:** Fictional instructional scenario

## Decision

A Phoenix AI hiring company is planning a European launch while facing bias claims and workforce concerns.

## Evidence

* The platform has processed more than two million applications.
* A civil-rights report alleges lower rankings for comparable HBCU candidates.
* The company plans to move 45 annotation jobs offshore.
* Sponsored employees fear retaliation if they report bias concerns.
