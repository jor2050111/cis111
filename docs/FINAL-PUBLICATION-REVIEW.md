## Structure and Alignment

* All 12 chapters use sections N.1 through N.7 in the required order.
* Each chapter has exactly three MLOs and a verbatim CLO alignment block.
* Each chapter has four Predict-Run-Explain activities.
* Each chapter has four Quick Checks with two or three questions.
* Each chapter has one three-part Skills Lab aligned to its MLOs.
* Each lab has exactly two Questions & Analysis prompts.
* Chapter 1 transcludes the shared rubric. Chapters 2-12 link to it.
* Each chapter has four review questions and 3-6 further reading sources.
* The student home page reproduces all 18 district CLOs verbatim.
* Navigation contains four Parts and 12 matching chapter titles.
* The glossary contains 221 unique definitions.

The structural audit reported zero failures.

## Content and Style

* Heading levels do not skip.
